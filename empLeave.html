The Employee Leave Management Web application is designed to efficiently manage employee information and leave requests. Employees can submit emergency or regular leave requests through the system, and administrators can review, approve, or reject these requests. The platform maintains all employee records, tracks leave history, and ensures streamlined communication between employees and management. This project provides a centralized solution for leave management, improving operational efficiency and record-keeping within the organization.
